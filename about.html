Our Story

Welcome to Zaika Zest, where tradition meets taste! Established in 2000, our restaurant has been a cherished destination for authentic Indian cuisine, serving up the rich flavors and vibrant spices of India in every dish.

At Zaika Zest, we take pride in offering a dining experience that celebrates the diversity of India’s culinary heritage. From classic North Indian dishes like butter chicken and tandoori to flavorful South Indian favorites like dosas and biryanis, our menu offers something for every palate.

Our chefs, with decades of experience, use fresh, high-quality ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to create meals that are not only delicious but also heartwarming. Whether you’re dining with family, enjoying a meal with friends, or celebrating a special occasion, we’re here to make every visit memorable.

Join us at Zaika Zest, where every meal is a celebration of Indian flavors and hospitality.
